About page architecture options for Dr. M's Experienced

The current page has strong credentials and deep context, but the first viewport is text-heavy and the proof points do not have a clear order. These options test three ways to make trust, expertise, and story easier to scan without flattening the content.

Research takeaways applied

Show the real person early

Headshot, location, and role should not disappear below the first mobile fold.

Make proof scannable

Credentials, publications, faculty, and media need a visible evidence hierarchy.

Reduce list fatigue

Dense clinical lists should be grouped into domains or milestones, not exposed all at once.
